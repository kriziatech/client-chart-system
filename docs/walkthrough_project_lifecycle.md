# Interior Touch: Project Lifecycle Walkthrough

This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough of the end-to-end project management process in the Interior Touch system, from the initial lead acquisition to final handover and project closure.

---

## Phase 1: Lead Acquisition & Discovery
**Objective:** Capture potential client details and initiate the relationship.

### 1. Getting a Lead (Stage 1: New Client)
*   **Action:** Navigate to the **Leads Board** and click **"+ Create New Lead"**.
*   **System Detail:** Fill in basic client name, phone, email, and property type.
*   **Outcome:** The client appears in the "Incoming Leads" pipeline. The project is at **Stage 1 (New Client)**.

### 2. Site Visit & Inspection (Stage 2: Site Visit)
*   **Action:** Open the lead details and navigate to the **"Workspace Overview"**.
*   **Input:** Find the **"Site Inspection & Discovery"** section and click **"Capture Site Info"**.
*   **Details:** Add site dimensions, property status (Ready/Under Construction), and take discovery notes.
*   **Outcome:** Once saved, the system automatically advances the project to **Stage 2 (Site Visit)**.

---

## Phase 2: Estimation & Financial Locking
**Objective:** Define the scope of work and secure financial commitment.

### 3. Create & Send Quote (Stage 3: Quotation)
*   **Action:** Navigate to the **"Estimates"** tab.
*   **Action:** Click **"Create BOQ (Bill of Quantities)"**.
*   **Technical Detail:** Add items from the master library or create custom line items with quantities and rates.
*   **Action:** Once the BOQ is finalized, click **"Finalize Version"** and share it with the client.
*   **Outcome:** The system marks the stage as **Stage 3 (Quotation)**.

### 4. Approve Quotes & Initial Credit (Stage 4: Credit)
*   **Action:** Once the client approves, record the advance payment in the **"Financials"** tab.
*   **Action:** Click **"Log New Payment"**, select "Credit", and enter the amount.
*   **Outcome:** Upon recording the first financial transaction, the system moves the project to **Stage 4 (Credit)**. The "Work" phase is now logically unlocked.

---

## Phase 3: Project Execution & Operations
**Objective:** Assign resources, set timelines, and execute tasks.

### 5. Team Allocation (Stage 5: Work Assigned)
*   **Action:** Go to the **Overview** tab.
*   **Action:** Locate the **"Personnel Management"** section.
*   **Input:** Assign a **Project Manager (PM)** and Site Supervisors. Click **"Save Team"**.
*   **Outcome:** With the team allocated, the system creates operational handles.

### 6. Operational Tasks & Milestones
*   **Action:** Navigate to the **"Execution"** (Tasks) tab.
*   **Action:** Create tasks with deadlines, priority levels, and assignees.
*   **Detail:** As the PM completes tasks, they mark them as "Completed".
*   **Outcome:** Project progress is tracked in real-time on the circular journey component.

### 7. Materials Purchase & Vendor Payments
*   **Tab:** **"Inventory"**.
*   **Purchase Action:** Click **"Record Purchase"** to log materials arriving at the site.
*   **Vendor Payment:** Go to the **"Financials"** tab, log a "Debit" payment, and associate it with a vendor.
*   **Outcome:** This tracks the project's cash outflow against the client's credit.

### 8. Locking the Timeline (Stage 6: Timeline)
*   **Action:** On the Overview tab, locate the **"Project Schedule"** section.
*   **Input:** Set the **Delivery Date**.
*   **Outcome:** The system locks the Gantt chart schedule and moves the project to **Stage 6 (Timeline)**.

---

## Phase 4: Quality Check & Handover
**Objective:** Finalize physical work and transit the project back to the client.

### 9. Final Visit & Checklist (Stage 7: Work Completed)
*   **Action:** Once all physical work is done, the PM performs a final site visit.
*   **Action:** Change the Project Status to **"Completed"** in the Edit Details section.
*   **Outcome:** The project moves to **Stage 7 (Work Completed)**.

### 10. Handover & Client Confirmation (Stage 8: Final Payment)
*   **Tab:** **"Handover"**.
*   **Action:** Complete the **"Handover Checklist"** (e.g., Deep cleaning, keys handed over, warranty docs).
*   **Action:** In the **"Finalization"** form, select the Warranty Period.
*   **Confirmation:** Ask the client to sign by typing their name in the **"Client Signature"** field.
*   **Outcome:** Click **"Finalize Portfolio Maturity"**.

### 11. Project Closure & Rewards
*   **Status:** The project is now at **Stage 8 (Final Payment / Closed)**.
*   **Closing:** Final accounts are settled in the Financials tab.
*   **Reward:** The PM's performance metrics (completion time vs. estimated time) are recorded in the system audit logs for quarterly rewards and performance bonuses.

---

**Dashboard Insight:** The **Project Lifecycle component** on the client page will now show all 8 nodes highlighted in green (or the final purple theme), indicating a successfully completed project.
